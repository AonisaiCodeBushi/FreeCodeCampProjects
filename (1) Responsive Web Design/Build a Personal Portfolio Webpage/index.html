About

I'm Dax Lloyd, a network administrator turned software developer. For the past few years I have worked as the only in-house IT specialist at Lanier Realty, what has become one of the largest Real Estate / Property Management companies in Savannah, GA. After playing a crucial role in researching and implementing new CRM and ERP systems to replace core legacy systems, I have realized that my true passion is in the ability to communicate information, ideas and stories to users through the use of technology. While I still enjoy networking- the the "envelope" that these stories flow through- my passion has turned to the "pen and the paper", where the user interacts with the information. I now have a desire to help organizations in communicating their vision to the rest of the world through code- to help be the pen and paper.

In order to pursue this, I have entered into what I now call "Dax Code Dojo", my personalized full-time, 12 week programming boot camp. While heavily utilizing Pluralsight, FreeCodeCamp, and other online resources, I have studied semantic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript libraries as well as the Microsoft technologies, C#, SQL, .NET Core and ASP.Net Core MVC. To advance my abilities with working with dev teams, I have also started studying Clean Code, OOP Design Patterns and Test Driven Development (TDD) as well as furthering my understanding of SCRUM Agile practices.

My future endeavors include gaining a greater understanding of Test Driven Development as well as evetually learn Xamarin, Unity 3d and Linux. Outside of work and study, you will find me spending time with my wife and little girl, reading or playing Code Combat, which has become one of my guilty pleasures...

I am currently seeking opporunities to work with Web development technologies, especially using Microsoft's .NET Core platform. If you beleive I may be a good fit for your organization or dev team, let's talk and get to know each other more!
